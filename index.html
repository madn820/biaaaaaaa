The King's Legacy

This project is a contemporary reinterpretation of the Golestan Palace, where history and modernity interact through a modern and nature-inspired design language. The aim is to create a space that blends respect for historical identity with an innovative and tranquil experience.

renders

The biomorphic forms of the tunnels are inspired by organic shapes found in nature, chosen to evoke a sense of invitation and fluid visitor movement. Beyond their aesthetic appeal, these forms contribute to a visual rhythm and a dynamic experience, naturally guiding the circulation path.

section

The green pathway and tree planting between the tunnels create a serene, nature-oriented atmosphere that contrasts elegantly with the white, minimal materials of the shops and the thermo-wood texture of the tunnels. This combination simultaneously conveys a sense of authenticity and futurism.

materials

The selected materials and forms enhance the intelligent interaction between urban space and greenery—where history, nature, and modern design are simultaneously experienced.
